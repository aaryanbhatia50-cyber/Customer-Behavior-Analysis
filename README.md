# Customer Shopping Behavior Analysis

## Project Overview

This project analyzes customer shopping behavior using Python, SQL, and Power BI to uncover purchasing patterns, customer preferences, revenue drivers, and business insights.

The workflow covers the complete analytics lifecycle, including data cleaning, feature engineering, exploratory data analysis (EDA), SQL-based business problem solving, and interactive dashboard development.

---

## Project Objectives

* Understand customer purchasing behavior
* Analyze revenue across customer segments
* Evaluate product and category performance
* Study customer subscription patterns
* Identify repeat buyer trends
* Discover factors influencing customer spending
* Build an interactive business intelligence dashboard

---

## Dataset Information

The dataset contains 3,900 customer purchase records and includes:

* Customer ID
* Age
* Gender
* Item Purchased
* Product Category
* Purchase Amount
* Location
* Size
* Color
* Season
* Review Rating
* Subscription Status
* Shipping Type
* Discount Applied
* Promo Code Usage
* Previous Purchases
* Payment Method
* Purchase Frequency

## Key Insights

### Customer Insights

* Loyal customers contribute a significant portion of overall revenue.
* Subscription status influences customer spending behavior.
* Certain age groups generate higher revenue than others.

### Product Insights

* Product performance varies significantly across categories.
* Highly rated products tend to attract more purchases.
* Discounts influence buying behavior for specific product categories.

### Business Insights

* Repeat customers are more likely to subscribe.
* Revenue concentration exists within specific customer segments.
* Customer retention presents opportunities for business growth.
